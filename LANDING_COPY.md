## FAQ

**Q: Is this actually free?**
A: Yes. The audit is completely free. No credit card, no account required. You only enter an email if you want to save and share your report.

**Q: How do you know if I'm on the wrong plan?**
A: We check your plan against your team size, seat count, and primary use case. If a cheaper plan from the same vendor covers your workflow, we flag it with the exact dollar difference.

**Q: Is my data stored anywhere?**
A: Your spend data stays in your browser until you choose to save your report. Only if you enter your email do we store the audit result — and personal details are never shown in the shareable public URL.

**Q: How current is the pricing data?**
A: All pricing is verified weekly against official vendor pricing pages. Every number in the audit engine cites a source URL and verification date.

**Q: What is Credex and why does it appear in my results?**
A: Credex sources discounted AI infrastructure credits from companies that overforecast. If your audit shows significant spend, we surface Credex as an option to reduce costs further — but only when the math genuinely supports it.
